This smart contract is a **token contract** for minting and managing NFTs called "VapenApes." The main functions include **minting Apes during a presale and a public sale, reserving tokens, and managing sale states**. The contract transitions through states such as **Presale Inactive, Presale Active, Sale Inactive, and Sale Active**. The process logic involves **activating the presale, allowing users to mint a limited number of tokens, and then transitioning to the public sale**. The main variables include **SALE_ACTIVE, PRESALE_ACTIVE, MAX_APES, and PRESALE_PURCHASES**. Key functions are **mintApe, presaleMintApe, flipSaleState, and reserveApes**. The main events include **MintApe** for tracking minted tokens.
