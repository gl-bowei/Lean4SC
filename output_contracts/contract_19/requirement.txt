This smart contract is a [membership contract] designed for managing airdrops and token purchases. Its main functions include [claiming airdrops, purchasing GCR tokens in exchange for USDC, and updating contract parameters by the owner]. The contract operates mainly in the [Airdrop Eligibility, Airdrop Claiming, and Token Purchasing] states. The process logic transitions from [checking airdrop eligibility to claiming airdrop, and then allowing eligible users to purchase tokens based on their tier]. The main variables include [owner, token contracts, exchange rate, airdrop amount, and tier thresholds]. The primary functions are [claimAirdrop, purchaseTokens, and administrative functions for owner management]. Important events include [ClaimAirdrop, Purchase, and WithdrawAll].
