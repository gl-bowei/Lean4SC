This smart contract is an [allocation pool contract]. The main functions include [adding pools for liquidity provision, depositing and withdrawing LP tokens, and rewarding users based on their contributions]. The main states of the contract include [Pool Creation, Active Pool, and Pending Withdrawal]. The process logic for the transition of states follows this sequence: [First, pools are created for accepting LP tokens. Users deposit tokens to earn rewards, and upon withdrawal requests, users may enter a pending state if there is a delay. Once the time has elapsed, they can claim their tokens. Rewards can also be harvested during these interactions]. The main variables include [allocRewardToken, allocPoolInfo, and allocUserInfo]. The main functions include [allocAddPool, allocDeposit, allocWithdraw, and allocClaimReward]. The main events include [AllocPoolCreated, AllocDeposit, and AllocWithdraw].
