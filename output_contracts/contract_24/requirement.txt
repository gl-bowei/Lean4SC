This smart contract is a [token contract] designed for minting and managing NFT tokens. Its main functions include [adding/removing users from various whitelist tiers, minting tokens, and admin minting]. The contract transitions between stages of [Whitelist Activation, Whitelisting Phase, and Minting Phase]. Initially, users are added to whitelists with specific allowances and prices. When minting begins, users can mint tokens based on their whitelist tier and the contract checks if the user meets conditions such as allowance and price. If conditions are met, tokens are minted, and allowances are deducted. Main variables include [totalSupply, whitelist mappings, and token prices]. Key functions are [mint, adminMint, and allowances management]. Significant events include [MintEvent].
