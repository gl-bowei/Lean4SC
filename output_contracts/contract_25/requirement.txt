This smart contract is a **staking rewards contract**. The main functions include **staking tokens, withdrawing stakes, claiming rewards, and updating rewards**. The contract's states involve **Staking, Reward Accumulation, and Withdrawal**. The logical process transitions as follows: **Users can stake their tokens, which calculates corresponding rewards over time. Users can claim rewards and withdraw their stakes at any time. Staking and rewards can be updated by the owner**. The main variables consist of **rewardRate, totalSupply, and balances**. The primary functions include **stake, withdraw, getReward, and updateRewardAmount**. Key events include **Staked, Reward Paid, and Reward Updated**.
