This smart contract is a [decentralized exchange (DEX) contract] for swapping two ERC20 tokens (tokenA and tokenB) and providing liquidity. The main functions include [swapping tokens, adding liquidity, and removing liquidity]. The contract manages states such as [Liquidity Added, Swapping, and Liquidity Removed]. The transition process involves [adding liquidity to the pool, allowing users to swap tokenA for tokenB and vice versa, and withdrawing liquidity from the pool]. The main variables include [tokenA, tokenB, and total supply of liquidity tokens]. The main functions include [swap, addLiquidity, and removeLiquidity]. The main events include [SwapAForB, SwapBForA, AddLiquidity, and RemoveLiquidity].
