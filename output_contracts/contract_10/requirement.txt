This smart contract is a [settlement contract]. The main functions include [settling payments between buyers and sellers, withdrawing ERC20 tokens, and changing the operator and Adam wallet]. The main states of the contract include [Payment Pending, Payment Settled, and Withdrawals]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [First, a buyer settles a payment by providing the seller's price, creator's royalty, and a deadline. The contract verifies the signature and checks the deadline and exclusivity. Upon successful validation, the payment is distributed to the seller, creator, and Adam wallet. After settlement, the state transitions to Payment Settled, allowing for withdrawals]. The main variables include [exclusives, operator, and adamWallet]. The main functions include [settle, withdrawERC20, changeOperator, and changeAdamWallet]. The main events include [Settled, ERC20Withdrawed, OperatorChanged, and AdamWalletChanged].
