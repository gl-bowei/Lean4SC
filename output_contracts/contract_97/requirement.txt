This smart contract is an [NFT minting contract]. Its main functions include [presale minting, public sale minting, airdrop, and token reservation]. The main states of the contract involve [Presale Period, Public Sale Period, Paused State, and Revealed State]. The logical process for state transitions is as follows: [First, set the presale and public sale start times. During the presale, eligible users can mint NFTs using a valid signature. Then, the public sale period begins, allowing anyone to mint NFTs until the max supply is reached. The contract can be paused or resumed by the owner, and NFTs can be revealed once ready]. Main variables include [maxSupply, price, presaleStart, publicStart, baseURI, revealed, and paused]. Main functions consist of [presaleMint, publicSaleMint, airdrop, and minting control functions]. Significant events include [token minted, sale started, and sale paused].
