This smart contract is a [token contract] designed for minting unique digital creatures. The main functions include [minting creatures in various pack sizes, transferring ownership, and checking minting eligibility]. The main states of the contract include [Minting Available and Minting Closed]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [Users can mint creatures based on selected options (single, nickel pack, dime pack, or dub pack) as long as the total supply does not exceed the limit. Ownership can be transferred, and minting can be checked for eligibility based on the selected option]. The main variables include [proxyRegistryAddress, nftAddress, baseURI, and GENTE_SUPPLY]. The main functions include [mint, canMint, and transferOwnership]. The main events include [Transfer].
