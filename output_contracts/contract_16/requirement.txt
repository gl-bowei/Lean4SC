This smart contract is an [NFT minting and management contract]. The main functions include [creating NFT moulds, minting NFTs, evolving and redeeming NFTs, and managing artwork data]. The main states of the contract include [Minting Active, Minting Closed, and NFT Evolved]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [Initially, an owner creates an NFT mould, enabling minting. Users can mint NFTs until the edition size is reached, at which point minting closes. NFTs can evolve to higher levels through authorized functions. Additionally, NFTs can be redeemed for specific attributes until they are fully redeemed]. The main variables include [NFTIndex, mintingActive, and various mappings for artwork data]. The main functions include [createNFTMould, ownerMint, evolveNFT, and redeemNFT]. The main events include [NewNFTMouldCreated, NewNFTCreatedFor, and RedeemNFT].
