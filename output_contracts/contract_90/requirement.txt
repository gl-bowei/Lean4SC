This smart contract is a **token contract** called "Star Coin" (SRC). Its main functions include **transferring tokens, approving allowances for spending, increasing or decreasing allowances, burning tokens, and checking balances and total supply**. The contract primarily operates in the states of **Initialized, Transferring Tokens, Allowance Management, and Token Burning**. The logical process for state transitions involves **initialization of total supply upon deployment, allowing users to transfer tokens, manage allowances, and burn tokens as required by the owner**. The main variables include **_balances, _totalSupply, _name, _symbol, and _decimals**. The main functions include **transfer, approve, transferFrom, increaseAllowance, decreaseAllowance, burn, and balanceOf**. The main events consist of **Transfer and Approval**, which are triggered during relevant state changes.
