This smart contract is a **staking contract** designed for users to stake HEX tokens. The main functions include **staking HEX, ending a stake, and retrieving stake and user information**. The contract transitions through the states of **Staking Active, Staking Ended, and Stake Withdrawn**. The process logic is as follows: **users initiate a stake by specifying the amount of HEX and the duration; they can later end the stake to retrieve their original amount plus any earned interest, which gets distributed appropriately.** The main variables include **user information, stake information, and associated fees**. The main functions include **stakeHex(), endStake(), updateUserData(), and updateStakeData()**. The key events are **StakeStarted and StakeEnded**.
