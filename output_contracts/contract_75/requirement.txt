This smart contract is a [token contract] designed for locking and unlocking tokens. The main functions include [locking tokens for specified addresses, unlocking tokens, and collecting tokens from locked and unlocked states]. The main states of the contract encompass [Tokens Locked, Tokens Unlocked, and Tokens Collected]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [Initially, the owner can lock specified amounts of tokens for various addresses. Once locked, these tokens cannot be used until they are explicitly unlocked by the owner. The owner can also collect tokens from both locked and unlocked addresses]. The main variables include [locked balances and token balances]. The main functions include [lockToken, unlockToken, collectFrom, and collectFromLocked].
