This smart contract is a **token contract**. The main functions include **transferring tokens, approving allowances, and burning tokens**. The main states of the contract revolve around **Token Ownership, Allowance Approval, and Token Burning**. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: **Initially, a token supply is allocated to the contract creator. Users can transfer tokens to each other and approve third parties to spend tokens on their behalf. Additionally, users can burn tokens, reducing their total supply and personal balance, either directly or by burning tokens from another user's allowance.** The main variables include **name, symbol, decimals, totalSupply, balanceOf, and allowance**. The main functions comprise **transfer, transferFrom, approve, approveAndCall, burn, and burnFrom**. The main events include **Transfer and Burn**.
