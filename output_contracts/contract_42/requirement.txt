This smart contract is a **token contract** designed for the **XinXian token** (XXC). Its main functions include **funding management, token allocation, and migration**. The contract transitions through several states: **Funding Not Started, Funding in Progress, and Funding Ended**. Initially, funding is started by the contract owner, allowing users to purchase tokens. Once funding is stopped, users can migrate their tokens to a new contract. The main variables include **ethFundDeposit, currentSupply, fundingStartBlock, and tokenExchangeRate**. Key functions include **startFunding, stopFunding, allocateToken, and migrate**. Important events are **AllocateToken, IssueToken, IncreaseSupply, and Migrate**.
