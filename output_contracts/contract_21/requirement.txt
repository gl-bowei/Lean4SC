This smart contract is a **token contract** specifically designed for minting NFTs using the ERC721A standard. The main functions include **controlling public sales, minting tokens, and managing supply limits**. The contract transitions through states such as **Public Sale Closed and Public Sale Open**, dictated by the owner's authority to toggle the sale status. The minting process requires specific conditions: users can mint tokens in limited quantities while ensuring the total supply does not exceed predefined limits. Key variables consist of **PRICE, MAX_PER_TXN, publicSale, maxSupply**, and **freeMaxSupply**. The main functions encompass **togglePublicSale, mint, adminMint, decreaseTotalSupply, and setBaseURI**. Significant events involve **public sale activation and minting activities**.
