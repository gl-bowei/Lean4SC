This smart contract is a **token contract** for managing the CorexToken. Its main functions include **transferring tokens, freezing accounts, and minting new tokens**. The contract's states involve **active trading**, where tokens can be transferred, and **minting control**, which determines if new tokens can be created. The transition logic is as follows: **users can transfer tokens unless an account is frozen; the owner can freeze accounts, mint new tokens, and toggle minting permissions**. The main variables include **sellPrice, buyPrice, frozenAccount mapping, and minting state**. The primary functions are **transfer, freezeAccount, mint, and changeMintingState**. Significant events emitted are **FrozenFunds, Mint, and CanMint**.
