This smart contract is a [Compound Farming contract]. The main functions include [depositing tokens into Compound, withdrawing tokens, managing fees, and handling vesting states]. The main states of the contract include [Normal Operation and Vesting State]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [Users can deposit tokens to earn interest, and withdraw tokens when not in vesting. The owner can initiate a vesting state to withdraw all tokens and claim rewards, after which users can only refund their shares. The contract can revert from vesting to normal operation]. The main variables include [token, cToken, pool, treasuryWallet, and communityWallet]. The main functions include [deposit, withdraw, vesting, refund, and revertVesting]. The main events include [SetTreasuryWallet, SetCommunityWallet, and SetNetworkFeeTier2].
