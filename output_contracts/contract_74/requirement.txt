This smart contract is an [NFT minting and distribution contract]. The main functions include [airdropping tokens, minting tokens, distributing funds to team members, and withdrawing balances]. The main states of the contract are [Minting Preparation, Minting Active, and Distribution]. The process logic for the transition of states is as follows: [Initially, the contract is set up for minting. When minting starts, users can mint tokens, which are then tracked. After minting, funds are distributed among team members based on predefined percentages, and members can withdraw their balances]. The main variables include [receiverAddresses, team, currentBatchMinted, and balances]. The main functions include [airdrop, mint, distributeFunds, sendFunds, and withdrawTeamMemberBalanceTo]. The main events include [Minted].
