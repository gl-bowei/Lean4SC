This smart contract is a **token contract** designed for managing a cryptocurrency called "iGniter" (symbol "INR"). Its main functions include **assigning initial and bounty addresses, transferring tokens, burning tokens, and processing service payments**. The contract transitions through stages starting with **initial address assignment**, followed by **bounty allocation**, and then proceeds to **token transfers and payments**. The main variables include **balances, initial supply, total supply**, and **burnt tokens**. Key functions are **transfer, approve, burn, and servicePayment**. Notable events consist of **Transfer, Burn, and Approval** to notify external observers of significant actions.
